Our Services

Our Integrated Building Security and Management System combines advanced security features and efficient building management capabilities to ensure safety, convenience, and operational efficiency. Utilizing fingerprint scanning and proximity card readers linked to network servers, our access control system regulates entry points and records attendance seamlessly.

In case of any threat, our direct alarm system immediately broadcasts alerts to security, civil defense, and law enforcement, with every action archived for review. Our wireless solutions provide flexible connectivity options, supporting internal and external sound systems for unrestricted movement and control. From multimedia systems covering outdoor and indoor spaces to e-services facilitating electronic transactions, our network offers seamless connectivity.

Telecommunication via optical fiber ensures fast, reliable connectivity for various signals, while our centralized building management system governs all inputs and outputs, implementing rules and strategies for optimal performance, including efficient visitor data recording and live communication capabilities at reception.
